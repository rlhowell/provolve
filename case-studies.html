Field Service Android Legacy Replacement

Field-service platform for 300 engineers

Context

A national lift and escalator service company operating 300 field engineers across 13 branches, with a mix of planned maintenance contracts and reactive callouts.

Challenge

Legacy systems created inconsistent processes across branches, heavy admin overhead, and poor visibility of engineer activity, job status, and contract performance.

Work delivered

Designed and built a synchronised Android application covering planned maintenance, reactive callouts, and aftercare contract management. Backend platform integrated with the existing contract and billing systems.

Outcome

Standardised operations across 13 branches, significantly reduced admin work, and gave management real-time visibility of job and engineer status.

Technologies & domains

Android Java Real-time sync Contract management Field service Lift & escalator industry

Construction Workforce Management Compliance

Construction workforce management platform — Hooped

Context

Specialist contractors in the construction sector managing thousands of operatives across multiple live projects, facing increasing regulatory pressure around compliance, competence verification, and site safety.

Challenge

Manual, paper-based processes for inductions, document verification, and compliance tracking created admin overhead, audit risk, and poor real-time visibility of who was on site and whether they were cleared to be there.

Work delivered

Designed and built Hooped — a workforce management platform replacing paperwork with a live digital system covering operative onboarding, inductions, competence verification, document management, and real-time site visibility for directors, site managers, and operatives.

Outcome

Thousands of operatives managed across multiple projects with significantly reduced admin, stronger compliance evidence, and real-time workforce visibility for management teams.

Technologies & domains

Flutter React iOS Android Workforce compliance Construction Document verification

HPC / Rust Data centre Low latency

HPC power-curtailment software

Context

A large-scale data-centre and HPC operator requiring real-time power management to respond to grid curtailment events and manage energy loads across infrastructure.

Challenge

Curtailment events require sub-second response times across distributed systems. Existing tooling could not meet latency and reliability requirements.

Work delivered

Built multithreaded, low-latency Rust software for power curtailment across data-centre operations, with secure gRPC communication and a Flutter management application for operations teams.

Outcome

Sub-millisecond curtailment response, reliable operation under load, and a clear management interface for the operations team.

Technologies & domains

Rust Flutter gRPC HPC Data centre Power management
